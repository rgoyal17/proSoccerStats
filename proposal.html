Stage 0: Topic Proposal

Problem Description

In my web app, I plan to design an effective way to search for soccer players and view information and statistics about them. Soccer is a team sport played with a spherical ball between two teams of eleven players with the objective to score by moving the ball beyond the goal line into the opposing goal. It is played in over 200 countries, making it the world's most popular sport but I feel not a lot of people follow it here in the US. I hope to maybe spread some awareness about this amazing game by providing useful information through my app. I am really passionate about soccer and feel like there exists no user-friendly app for soccer fans to look up their favorite players to get some information about them other than googling each player. This app will enable users to easily get all of the information about any professional soccer player in one place.

The most popular online source for getting information about soccer is currently through this website by ESPN. Although this is a very informative site, I feel that it is trying to give too much information at once such as news, fixtures, scores, etc. and does not focus on specific player stats. It is the same for other sources like WhoScored. There is no web app particularly for just player information and statistics, most focus on teams.

App Description

I plan to make a web app for soccer fans and people hoping to find data about their favoite soccer players. It can be used to look up professional soccer players and view information and statistics about them such as teams played for, number of goals scored, matches played, fouls committed, etc. Search results can be filtered based on attributes like country, league, team, etc. Based on statistics, you can compare players. A user can also choose its favorite players and make their own teams. There will also be a login option so that users' favorite players and teams are saved. The player data is gathered from this API source and the stats are updated frequently.

Rather than trying to provide too much information about soccer like fixtures, teams, scores, etc., my app will only focus on player information and statistics. In this way, my app will be user-friendly and not too complicated. By providing all this information in one place, and making it easy to access, my app will be helpful in spreading awareness and gaining more knowledge about this game.

App Functionality

A user can use the web app in the following way:

  1. There is a search box where the user can enter the player's name.
  2. If the user doesn't know the player's name, players can be looked up by filtering on attributes like country, team, league, etc.
  3. After selecting a player, all the information and statistics about that player will be displayed.
  4. Users can select two or more players two compare their stats.
  5. A player can be marked as favorite but will require to login first to access this feature.
  6. There is also a feature to create your own team and favorite players can be added to it.
  7. Team formations can be edited, player roles and positions can be assigned or changed in your team.
